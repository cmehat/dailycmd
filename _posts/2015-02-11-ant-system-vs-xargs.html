--- layout: post title: "awk 'system()' vs xargs" date: 2015-02-11 12:22:00 +0000 permalink: /2015/02/ant-system-vs-xargs.html tags: ["cmdline", "kill", "awk", "xargs"] author: "cm" --- {% raw %} Two different syntaxes for processing a command over a list of arg.

Let's take for example, the list of PID you want to kill(all the processes matching "toto") :

ps | grep toto | awk -F' ' '{print $2}' | xargs kill -9

is equivalent to

ps | grep toto | awk -F' ' '{system("kill -9 "$2}'

note that the space at the end of the string "kill -9 " is important since the $2 will be concatenated. {% endraw %}

On y trouve les trucs suivants :

  • =IMAGE(A2), where A2 must contain a valid URL


  • =GOOGLETRANSLATE(A2, “en”,”it”)

  • =SPLIT(A2; “;”)

  • =hyperlink(“URL”,”Title”) =HYPERLINK(A2, B2)

  • =DAYS360(A2,B2) with A2 and B2 containing dates
